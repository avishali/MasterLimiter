What this changes vs. now: buttons get a soft gradient + 1px top highlight (not flat), a 7px radius (less boxy), and a clear on-state — teal border + inner glow + lit accent dot, vs. a neutral idle. Selectors become compact segmented toggles. Palette tightens the teal and the text/muted hierarchy for legibility; warning is a warm coral, not a hard red. Knob ring is a cleaner two-tone arc with a soft accent glow. React freely — tweak any hex, the button on-state treatment, radius, or knob style, and the adjusted version becomes the 16b implementation spec.
